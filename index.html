    Define & Tailor Your Restaurant's Needs

    Restaurant Concept

    Develop a defining theme for the restaurant, understanding the target market and determining: price point, casual/formal, what type of food, how you vary from competition around you.

    Design and Layout

    There are many fundamental elements to designing a restaurant and setting your ideal ambiance, including: lighting, ventilation, noise control, apparel of staff, silverware, glassware, flatware, kitchen concept, capacities, etc.

    Menu Design

    Complete menu design from recipe books, lunch and dinner menus, seasonal cocktail lists, banquet menus and buffets. Developing a solid food menu is the core of any successful restaurant. People may come back after back service, but most do not return after poor food! This also includes the physical design of menus to match the ambiance of the restaurant.
